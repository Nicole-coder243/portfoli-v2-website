Weather App

This dynamic Weather Web Application provides real-time weather conditions for any city, elegantly combining data from two external APIs: OpenWeather Map and Unsplash. Users can simply type a city name to instantly receive current weather details, including temperature, humidity, and wind information, alongside a visually striking background image of that city related to its current weather conditions.
